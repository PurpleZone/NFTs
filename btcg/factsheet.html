Here are the (immutable) facts about the BTC-G coin

This BTC-G coin is a wrapped bitcoin, minted to track and negate the emission generated from the mining of the original bitcoin. We ensure the coin is carbon neutral by guaranteing the right amont of living trees is planted and protected until the total emitted carbon is sequestred back in the ground.

(note: remember a transaction on the bitcoin main chain emit 417kg of CO₂, so please plant 1 to 3 tree if you want to move the bitcoin arround)
